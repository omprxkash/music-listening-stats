## How this list stays here

This file is hand-kept, and the same albums live as data in [`data/favorite-albums.json`](data/favorite-albums.json) so the site can render them. When my Apple Music playlist link is ready, it'll sit at the top of `data/apple-music.json` and show up under **My Favourites → My playlists** on the site.
